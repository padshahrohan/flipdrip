Flipdrip Seller Loyalty Program 🛒

Prerequisites 🛠️:

  • Before joining the programme, you must set up a metamask wallet and enable the metamask extension in your browser (https://metamask.io/download/)
  • Your wallet address is your unique address that will be used for all Flipdrip transactions
  • In case you have an existing wallet, ensure you are signed in to your wallet from your metamask browser extension

Eligibility 🎯:

  • Only registered sellers on Flipkart are eligible to participate in the Flipdrip loyalty program.
  • Sellers must comply with Flipkart's terms of service and community guidelines.

Token Allocation 🎁:

  • Upon registration, sellers will receive an initial allocation of 5,000 "Flipdrips" (FDs) as a welcome bonus.
  • Additional FDT can be earned through various program activities.

Earning FDs 🏆:

  • Sellers can earn FDs from Flipkart:
  • Customers who have accumulated sufficient FDs can purchase your product using FDs. In that case, FDs will be directly credited from the customer's wallet to your wallet.

FD Request and Fee 💰:

  • If you ever run out of FDs, you can request for FDs through the Flipdrip portal. You will be charged a fee of INR 5,000 for 100 FDs. You can only request for FDs if you have exhausted your current FD balance.

FD Utility 🛒:

  • FDs can be used within the Flipdrip ecosystem for redeeming - purchasing products after accumulating sufficient FDs.

Governance 🗳️:

  • Sellers may have a say in program governance through voting mechanisms, helping to shape the future of Flipdrip.

Compliance 📜:

  • Sellers must adhere to any tax or legal requirements related to owning and using FDs within their respective jurisdictions.

Updates 📢:

  • Flipkart reserves the right to update or modify the Flipdrip program rules and tokenomics with prior notice to participating sellers.

By adhering to these rules and tokenomics, Flipkart's Flipdrip loyalty program aims to incentivize and reward sellers for their contributions to the platform while promoting transparency, fairness, and engagement within the ecosystem.
